Frequent Inquiries

Do you travel for destination weddings?
Yes, we travel Pan-India and internationally. We manage on-site rehearsals and coordinate directly with your wedding planners for stage logistics.
Our family has two left feet. Can you help?
Absolutely. Our expertise lies in making non-dancers look graceful and feel confident. We tailor the choreography to suit every individual's comfort level.
How early should we book?
We take a limited number of weddings per season to maintain our quality. We recommend booking at least 3-4 months in advance.
